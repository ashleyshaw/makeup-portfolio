# Project Governance

This project follows an open governance model. Major decisions are made by consensus among core maintainers, with input from the community.

## Maintainers

- @ashleyshaw (Lead)

## Decision Making

- Proposals are discussed in issues or discussions.
- Major changes require approval from the lead maintainer.
- Community input is encouraged and considered.

## Dispute Resolution

- If consensus cannot be reached, the lead maintainer has final say.
